🧾 Useful Notes and Examples

  • Bold vs Strong:
    <b> just makes text bold.
    <strong> adds semantic importance to the content.
  • Italics vs Emphasis:
    <i> is purely stylistic (e.g., technical terms, foreign words).
    <em> signals emphasis in meaning, useful for screen readers.
  • Line Breaks: Use <br> to break lines in plain text emails or inside paragraphs:
    Hello,<br>How are you today?
  • Strikethrough: Use <del> when showing that content was intentionally removed.
    Use <s> for stylistic strikethroughs.
  • Highlighting: Use <mark> to draw attention to specific content (usually yellow background).
  • Subscript/Superscript:
    Subscript: H2O
    Superscript: x2
